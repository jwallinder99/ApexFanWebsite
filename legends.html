A Look at some of the Legends

Each legend in the game has their own set of abilities, which are unique to that particular legend. Each legend also has a voice actor behind them that brings them to life. The game has a ping communication system where you are able to ping different items/ points of interest, and depending on which legend you are playing, that legend will have their own custom voiceline for that particular callout.

Wraith

Interdimensional Skirmisher

Wraith is able to teleport into the void for a few seconds before returning back to the world. While she is in the void, she moves alot faster and is able to reappear in a new location.

Wraith is able to hear another version of herself in an alternate dimension. This alternate version of herself is able to see her in a 3rd person perspective, and alerts her when someone is aiming at her that she can't see

Wraith is able to make a portal starting from one location, and she is able to place it at another location. When she places the first entrance for the portal, she gains an enormous speed boost, and has this until the other side of the portal is placed. From there, Wraith, enemies, and her teammates can use the portal. The portal stays up for 30 seconds
